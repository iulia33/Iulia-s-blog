A few words about me

Born and raised in Zalau, I headed for Cluj when I was 19 years old. It was a very sad moment for me. I was leaving behind a world I thought I'll never find again. After just two weeks in Cluj everything was different. I was already loving my student life. I have studied and graduated the Faculty of Economics and I loved it. After eight years of living in Cluj, my boyfriend (husband now) and I decided to move to Timisoara.

Even though the accomodation in Timisoara was not an easy thing, the whole experience of living there was something totally unexpected. I have developed a lot, I've discovered things about me I never knew existed. Also, I have met and made a lot of friends and I've traveled in many places. Two years ago I gave birth to a beautiful baby girl. On a professional level, I have evolved a lot but I've got to a point where I decided that I need a new challenge.

At the end of 2015, we started thinking of moving (back) to Cluj. Slowly, the idea became reality, and here we are: back in Cluj, with new challenges ahead, but also new opportunities.
